Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to frequent pre-sale questions asked by customers

  • Can I use a third party escrow service to buy a domain listed on BrandDo?

    Yes, it's possible to achieve a transaction using a third-party escrow service Eg. Escrow.com, GoDaddy or DomainAgents. However, you need to agree to pay the fees required by these platforms.

  • Are there any additional fees besides the domain price?

    No. You only pay the price shown on the website or the agreed price, there are no extra charges. Also, BrandDo offers the escrow service for free.

  • What happens after I pay a domain name on BrandDo website?

    Once we receive and verify your payment, one of our agents will be in touch with you to coordinate the transfer process.

  • What about the transfer time frame?

    The transfer can take anywhere between 1 to 7 days, depending on the current registar. Typically, if you choose to use the same registrar where the domain currently resides, the transfer is the quickest since the domain only changes accounts. If you choose a different registrar, there is an ICANN-mandated waiting period of 5 to 7 days that starts after the current owner has approved the transfer.

  • What do I get when I buy a name from BrandDo?

    When buying a name from BrandDo

    1. You become the new owner of the domain name and it remains listed in your own registrar account with full control on it, as long as the yearly registration is maintained through the registrar.

    2. You get the logo and all graphics we designed for this brand name in Adobe Illustrator or Photoshop format. If you don't want to use this logo design it's up to you. This will not affect the domain sales price.

  • Do you lease the domain names?

    No. We don't lease domain names and no recurring costs will be charged after the sale.

  • Can your team help me find a catchy business name?

    Our team would be happy to give you 20 catchy and captivating brand name suggestions for free in 24 hours.
    You can also visit our website at www.branddo.com to find more brandable domain names for sale.

  • Do your names include any trademark rights?

    No. Since we do not trademark our names, none of them include trademark rights. When purchasing a brand from us you are only buying the domain and the ownership rights to the logo design artwork that we provide with it.
